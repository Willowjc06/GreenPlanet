Green Planet is a new resaurant located in the heart of Bristol city centre, dedicated to serving you fresh, delicious, organic and all sustainable food. Our aim is to provide our costumers with a unique dining experience, dedicating our resources to those who often get forgotten about within the food industry.

Our menu was carefully crafted to include a varierty of vegan cuisines, with the aim of showing our customers that vegan food can be exciting. We have various different dishes on the menu, including our one-of-a-kind all-vegan desserts. You can explore our menu here.
